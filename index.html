CodeBox

Organized by Siemens Gamesa, we had to find a way to make learning easy for students and teachers. We developed this app, a way to teach maths with augmented reality, via problem solving in real world. Programing works in a scratch-like game for the younger ones.

Caecus

Organized by King, we had to develop a game which encourages inclusion. In our game, you take control of a blind person, who will fear the enviroment, sounds heard, and all kind of events. The only things you see is what you hear, and what you touch.

Forgotten Books

Forgotten books, a stealth game where you are in a lost library, looking for real lost books. You end up finding monsters that you need to hide from.

Baby King

Due to destiny, a baby has taken over a kingdom. You will have to make decisions for him, that keep him happy but do not anger the population.

Pentagrammed

Become the best musician in the world, playing many instruments in the right time when song demands it. A fun platform game where you need to think the way to play the many instruments you have in the map in time. Battle versus 3 unique bosses and enviroments
